About The Malloy Foundation

Founded in 1983 by Marjorie Malloy, Jane Black Kennedy, Joanne Black Jones, and Susan Black Tiger. The primary beneficiaries are The Orange County Chapter of the American Red Cross; The First United Methodist Church of Orange, Texas; St. Mary’s Catholic Church of Orange, Texas; and The Little Cypress-Mauriceville High School performing and fine arts scholarship. The Foundation's Board may also approve smaller grants to other organizations described in Sections 501(c)(3) and 509(a)(1) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1954.

Since 1983, the Foundation has donated over $6,750,000 to local charities.

Make A Grant Request

Due September 30

Requests may be made by e-mailing or mailing a one (1) page letter, along with a copy of your 501 (c) (03) tax status. The letter should describe the work of your organization, the amount requested, and how the money will be used. If submitting by e-mail, please send the requested information as a .doc or .pdf file attachment.
